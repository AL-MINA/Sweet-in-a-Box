Learn more about us!

The story of our business begins with a love for baking that spans generations. We began by learning to make pastries and emphasizing the importance of using the finest ingredients and time-honored baking techniques. We spent hours experimenting with ingredients, ratios, and baking times, scouting the world for the highest-quality ingredients.


As our recipes matured, our commitment to quality grew, and we became known for their signature taste, texture, and consistency. We decided to share these delicious creations with a broader audience and started promoting our cookies exclusively through Facebook. As demand for our cookies grew, we decided to start supplying local coffee shops. We worked closely with coffee shop owners to understand their unique needs and preferences, ensuring our cookies perfectly complemented their offerings.


Through hard work, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, we earned the trust of our first coffee shop clients and became a recognized supplier.


Today, we are proud to be a trusted partner to numerous coffee shops, serving our delectable cookies alongside the finest coffee and beverages. Our journey from a learner baker to a beloved supplier in the coffee shop industry is a testament to our dedication to quality and our enduring love for the art of baking. We look forward to continuing this delicious journey and sharing our passion for cookies with you and your customers.


Why Cookies are attractive?

Cookies are universally appealing, evoking feelings of comfort and nostalgia. They serve as symbols of hospitality and togetherness. Their versatility allows for a wide array of flavors and textures, catering to diverse taste preferences. Conveniently portioned, cookies are ideal for quick snacks or as accompaniments to beverages. Engaging multiple senses, the aroma and visual appeal of cookies enhance their allure. Cookies are commonly associated with celebrations, contributing to the joy of various occasions. Their charm lies in bringing people together, providing moments of indulgence, and evoking happiness. Overall, cookies' enduring and universal appeal makes them irresistibly attractive.

History of Baking

The history of baking can be traced back to ancient civilizations, with the first evidence of bread-making dating to around 14,000 years ago. The process of baking has evolved significantly over time, from early methods involving flatbreads cooked on hot stones to the development of ovens and leavened bread in ancient Egypt. The Romans refined baking techniques, and during the Middle Ages, baking became a respected profession in Europe. The introduction of baking powder in the 19th century revolutionized baking, allowing for the creation of cakes, cookies, and other sweet treats. Today, baking is both a culinary art and a science, with a rich history that has shaped the way we enjoy baked goods.
